# 🛍️ Myntra Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This project showcases an interactive **Myntra Sales Dashboard** built to analyze and visualize sales performance, product distribution, brand insights, customer engagement, and pricing trends. The dashboard helps in deriving actionable insights for better business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall product and brand distribution
* Track total revenue and discounts
* Evaluate brand-wise performance
* Understand customer engagement (footfall)
* Explore category-wise trends and ratings

---

## 📊 Dashboard Metrics

* **Total Products:** 21,605
* **Total Brands:** 2,139
* **Total Revenue:** ₹1,28,12,37,476+
* **Average Rating:** 4.1 ⭐
* **Average Discount:** 44.0%

---

## 📈 Key Visualizations

### ⭐ Average Rating of Brands

* Displays rating comparison across brands
* Helps identify top-performing brands

### 🏆 Top 10 Brands by Product Count

* Shows brands with the highest number of products
* Indicates brand dominance in the marketplace

### 👣 Total Footfall

* Pie chart representing customer distribution
* Highlights user engagement levels

### 📦 Product Category Analysis

* Shows number of product categories by brand
* Helps understand product diversity

### 📊 Product Category Rating Count

* Displays number of ratings per category
* Identifies most popular product categories
